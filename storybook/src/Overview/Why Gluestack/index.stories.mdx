# Why gluestack?

gluestack started as an internal project at GeekyAnts to bring standardization of practices across multiple web & mobile projects.

If you’re looking for why we need a full-stack framework. You might refer to the [rant blog post](https://sanket.info/writing/rant-what-the-stack) by Sanket Sahu, which discusses the problems of modern-day full-stack development.
